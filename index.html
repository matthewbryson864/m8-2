Our Team

Meet the Chefs behind M8s

Holly Barcelona

Holly's culinary journey began in her teenage years. After graduating High School in 1995, she joined the Marine Corps and completed her Military Police training. Stationed in Okinawa, Japan, for two years, Holly developed an interest in Japanese cuisine, mastering sashimi, sushi rolls, and renowned Japanese-style fried rice. Upon leaving the Marines, Holly entered the restaurant industry, later deciding to pursue her culinary dream. She excelled in culinary school, earning accolades like the Dean's List, President's List, and perfect attendance. A chef instructor recognized her dedication and recommended her as a line cook at a high-end restaurant, where she worked under Chef Mark Swomley for 1.5 years, including her externship.

Holly's culinary journey then led her to Delaware, where she became a sous chef at Home Grown CafÃ©. She later moved back to South Carolina, serving as staff chef at Nantahala Outdoor Centers' Chattooga Outpost, where she transitioned from raft guiding to cooking. She eventually became the executive chef and general manager at Relias Garden, as well as the kitchen manager at Rivers End Rest during her 6-year tenure with NOC. A significant life changing event brought Holly to a new town, where she met her future husband, Vincent, and decided to make it her permanent home. After getting married and having two children, the family spent four more years in Okinawa, Japan. Holly later returned to the United States, becoming an executive chef at Belmont Village for three years before relocating to South Carolina following her husband's passing in 2021.

Today, Holly brings her extensive culinary experience to Table 301, where she has dedicated over a decade to her career.

Alex Vanhook

Alex VanHook is a chef driven by a culinary passion. He embarked on his culinary journey at 14 years old as a dishwasher in a small-town diner. He swiftly moved up to a fry cook during high school, graduating with honors from a culinary arts program under Chef Hoffman. Alex later joined Jianna, an Italian restaurant based in Greenville South Carolina. Over six years, he rose to Senior Sous Chef. Seeking fresh experiences, he joined Jones Oyster Company and crossed paths with his future business partner, Holly.

Recognized for his culinary dedication, Alex became a Sous Chef at Table 301 Catering Company. However, his true passion lay in sharing his culinary creations directly with diners in a more intimate setting. After six months, Alex and Holly launched M8s Private Chef In-Home Dining Experience. Their venture offers a personalized five-course dining experience in the comfort of diners' homes, complete with the freedom to enjoy their chosen wines and spirits at a fraction of the typical cost.

Join Alex in this exciting journey to "taste the difference" with M8s Private Chef In-Home Dining Experience.
